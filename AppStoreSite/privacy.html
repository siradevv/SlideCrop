Privacy Policy

SlideCrop · Last updated: February 28, 2026

What SlideCrop does

SlideCrop processes photos you select to detect, straighten, and crop presentation slides.

Photo library access

SlideCrop requests access to your photo library only so it can read selected photos and save processed outputs. You can choose full or limited access in iOS settings.

Data collection

SlideCrop does not require account sign-in, does not run advertising trackers, and does not sell personal data.

Data sharing

SlideCrop does not sell or broker your personal information.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@slidecrop.app.
